About Coldbot Coffee

I started Coldbot Coffee in April, 2015. While working as a craft beer brewer, I began to seek out unique flavor experiences to expand my palate. During this journey I discovered many tasty things made by skilled artisans. Creations such as wine, cheese, molecular gastronomy, cigars and…. COFFEE! The experience of coffee and flavors that I discovered kept me coming back for more. I quickly began to delve into the nuanced brewing science aspects of specialty coffee. The cold brewing method was familiar because of my previous experiences steeping coffee and other unique ingredients in beer at cold temperatures. The concentrated flavors, shelf life and ability to make large batches resonated with me.

I began to seek out unique coffee from around the world and experiment with the flavors I could extract into my cold brew. Turns out that it requires a lot of coffee to investigate the many brewing techniques and variables. My demand for more coffee and do it myself attitude sparked an interest in small batch home roasting. This fortuitous path lead me to purchase raw green coffee beans and a home roaster from a local coffee importer, Bodhi Leaf Coffee.

The talented staff at Bodhi Leaf have been very helpful and kind enough to share their knowledge of coffee. When I decided that I wanted to start Coldbot Coffee, it was a natural fit to work with Bodhi Leaf for sourcing the highest quality coffee and experience in roasting. Their Diedrich roaster is highly acclaimed for using an infrared heat source. This provides for a cleaner roast that helps to highlight the unique flavors in specialty coffee.

My vision is to build a “Coffee Brewery”. The support of my loyal customers will serve to finance a facility for roasting, cold brewing, coffee education and a cafe. Ready to drink cold brew coffee on tap, in cans and bottles.
