About Coldbot Coffee

I discovered quality coffee while working as a craft beer brewer. My journey to discover new flavors and ingredients to use in beer lead me to specialty coffee. I was already well versed in steeping coffee in beer at cold temperatures. Naturally, this lead me to seek out the best coffee I could find and use it to make cold brew.

Soon I was making batch after batch, tweaking different variables to learn about the process. My search for quality research in the area of cold brew coffee revealed that there was actually very little research done in this area. Those first few steps into the “rabbit hole” was all that was needed for me to be hooked.

What makes us different?

The transparency of our product is proven by our commitment to sharing knowledge about the ingredients and processes used. Our website serves to educate consumers about the ease of making cold brew coffee using the best coffee beans from around the world.
